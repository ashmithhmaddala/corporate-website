Our Hiring Process

We try to keep things simple and respectful of your time.

  1. Application Review – We read every application. No AI screening.
  2. Intro Call – 30 minutes to get to know each other and answer your questions.
  3. Technical/Role Assessment – A paid take-home project or working session relevant to the role.
  4. Team Conversations – Meet 2-3 team members you'd be working with.
  5. Offer – We move fast once we find the right person.

The whole process typically takes 2-3 weeks. We always provide feedback, whether or not we move forward.
