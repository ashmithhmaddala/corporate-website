The problem

Secrets end up everywhere except where they should be.

Slack threads. Notion docs. .env files that haven't been rotated in months. Shared password vaults with no audit trail. When someone leaves, you're never 100% sure what they still have access to.

How Vault works

One source of truth for your team's secrets.

01

Centralized by default

Every secret your team needs lives in Vault. Database URLs, API keys, certificates – encrypted at rest with AES-256.

02

Access that makes sense

Control who sees what at the environment level. Juniors get development. Seniors get production. Revoke instantly when someone leaves.

03

Sync anywhere

Pull secrets with our CLI. Sync to Vercel, AWS, or GitHub Actions automatically. No more copy-pasting from a shared doc.

04

Complete audit trail

Every access logged. Every change tracked. When compliance asks who touched production secrets last Tuesday, you'll know.
